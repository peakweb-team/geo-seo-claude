## The 6 Biggest Problems (And What They Cost You)

### 1. AI Systems Literally Can't Read Your Website

**The Issue:** Your Wix website loads content via JavaScript. AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) receive mostly empty HTML with framework code — your actual content, prices, portfolio, and expertise information is invisible to them.

**What This Costs You:**
When a hunter asks ChatGPT "What's a good taxidermist in Chugiak?", the AI has almost nothing to work with from your site. It can see your page titles and meta descriptions, but not your 23 years of experience, your Animal Artistry training, or your sheep specialization.

**Business Impact:** You're essentially invisible to the 30-40% of potential customers now using AI for research.

**Fix Complexity:** Medium — requires adding content to your HTML or using Wix's SEO settings strategically.

---

### 2. Zero Structured Data (Schema Markup)

**The Issue:** Your website has literally zero schema markup — no LocalBusiness, no Organization, no Service, nothing. This is like having a business card with no name, phone number, or address.

**What This Costs You:**
- AI systems can't identify *what* your business is or *where* it's located
- Google can't show your business in local map results
- You miss rich result opportunities (star ratings, prices, etc.)
- AI has no way to verify you're a real business entity

**Current Schema Score:** 5/100 (Critical)

**Fix Complexity:** Medium — requires adding JSON-LD code (we've provided templates).

---

### 3. No Contact Information Visible to AI

**The Issue:** Your phone number, email, and full address aren't visible in the initial HTML that AI crawlers receive. They load dynamically via JavaScript, which AI can't execute.

**What This Costs You:**
When someone asks "How do I contact Wild Reflections taxidermy?", AI systems have nothing to cite. They can't recommend calling you if they don't know your number.

We found your address from the Alaska business registry (22444 Northwoods Dr, Chugiak, AK 99567), but AI systems shouldn't have to work that hard.

**Fix Complexity:** Low — add contact info to static HTML or schema markup.

---

### 5. No llms.txt File

**The Issue:** There's a new standard file (like robots.txt for AI) called llms.txt that tells AI systems what your business is about. You don't have one.

**What This Costs You:**
When AI systems want to understand your business quickly, there's no authoritative source. They have to guess from fragments they can find — and often guess wrong or cite competitors instead.

**Fix Complexity:** Very Low — 15 minutes to create and upload.

---

### 6. Homepage Meta Description Has a Typo

> Current: "Stunningly realistic taxid**emy**. Based in Chugiak..."

**What This Costs You:**

- May cause AI to question the accuracy of other content
- Hurts click-through rates in search results

**Fix Complexity:** Very Low — 1 minute in Wix.
