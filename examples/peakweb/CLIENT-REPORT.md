## The 6 Biggest Problems (And What They Cost You)

### 1. ❌ AI Systems Don't Know Who You Are

**The Issue:** Your business has no "identity" in AI databases (no Wikipedia page, no LinkedIn company page, minimal online presence beyond your website).

**What This Costs You:**
When someone asks ChatGPT "Who does sprinkler repair in Denver?", the AI doesn't recognize you as a legitimate business entity. It's like not being in the phone book.

**Business Impact:** Lost leads from the 30-40% of people now using AI to research local services.

---

### 2. ❌ Your Website Has No Dates On Anything

**The Issue:** Not a single page shows when it was published or last updated. Your testimonials have no dates. Your content could be from 2015 or 2025 — AI systems can't tell.

**What This Costs You:**
AI systems assume undated content is stale and won't cite it. When someone asks about 2026 sprinkler prices or current services, your pages are skipped as potentially outdated.

**Real-World Example:**
Customer asks: "What does sprinkler winterization cost in Denver in 2026?"
AI answer: *Cites your competitor's blog from 2025 with dates, ignores your page with no date.*

---

### 3. ❌ 20+ Testimonials Are Hidden From AI

**The Issue:** You have 20+ customer testimonials on your site, but they're not marked up in a way AI systems can understand. It's like having great Yelp reviews but they're written on paper in a drawer.

**What This Costs You:**
- No star ratings show up in Google search results (competitors with ratings get more clicks)
- AI systems can't verify your reputation because they can't "see" your reviews
- You're missing the trust signals that convert browsers into callers

**Fix Time:** 30 minutes to add the proper code. Immediate impact.

---

### 4. ❌ Your Content Is Generic Marketing Copy

**The Issue:** Your service pages say things like "professional sprinkler repair services" but don't include specific data, pricing, timelines, or Denver-specific insights.

**What AI Systems Want:**
- "We've serviced 1,200+ systems annually since 2011"
- "Denver winterization costs $85-$150 depending on zone count"
- "Denver's clay soil requires 1.5-2.0 inches of water weekly in summer"

**What This Costs You:**
When AI systems answer specific questions, they cite websites with *data*, not marketing language. Your 25 years of experience exists but isn't documented anywhere AI can find it.

---

### 5. ❌ Missing Technical Files AI Systems Look For

**The Issue:** Your site is missing two critical files:
- **No sitemap** (the map AI uses to find all your pages) — returns error 404
- **No llms.txt** (the instruction file that tells AI what your site is about)

**What This Costs You:**
It's like having a store with no directory and the lights off. AI systems have to guess what you do and what pages matter most. Many give up and cite competitors instead.

**Fix Time:** 30 minutes total. Templates are ready to copy-paste.

---

### 6. ❌ Business Age Inconsistency Hurts Trust

**The Issue:** Your website says "25+ years in business" but BBB shows business started in 2011 (14 years ago).

**What This Costs You:**
AI systems fact-check claims. When they find conflicting information, they either:
- Skip citing you entirely (trust issue)
- Cite your competitor who has consistent information
- Mention the discrepancy in their answer (hurts credibility)

**The Fix:** Clarify that *you* (Ramon) have 25+ years experience, but *the incorporated business* started in 2011. Be consistent everywhere.
